The International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) is a set of symbols that linguists use to describe the sounds of spoken languages.

This site allows you to press buttons for IPA symbols on your phone (or other device) and have them typed on your computer (provided you have it configured correctly).

Alteratively, you can press the buttons and copy and paste the output (if you are not logged in).


If the site doesn't work, make sure you haven't been timed out (this usually happens after a little more than an hour of inactivity).  If you have been timed out, simply press Renew and you should be good to go.  If that doesn't work, restart the client, logout, and enter the new key.
